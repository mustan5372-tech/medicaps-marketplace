## Usage Tips

### Best Times to Post
- 📱 Morning: 8-10 AM (breakfast time)
- 🌙 Evening: 6-8 PM (after work)
- 🌃 Night: 9-11 PM (leisure time)

### Best Days
- 📅 Tuesday-Thursday (highest engagement)
- 🎉 Weekends (more free time)
- 🎊 Festival seasons (special offers)

### Posting Strategy
1. **Rotate prompts** - Don't repeat same message
2. **Mix content** - Alternate between features, stories, offers
3. **Use hashtags** - Helps reach more people
4. **Add visuals** - Screenshots, videos, infographics
5. **Engage** - Reply to comments, answer questions
6. **Track results** - See which prompts work best

### Customization
- Replace [Download Link] with actual link
- Update [DATE] with actual dates
- Adjust prices based on your market
- Add local language versions
- Include your app name/logo
